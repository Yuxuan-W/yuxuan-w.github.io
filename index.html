    I am Yuxuan Wang, currently a second-year Ph.D. candidate from MReal Lab in Nanyang Technological University (NTU) supervised by Prof. Hanwang Zhang. Before that, I was a Master of Science student in Show Lab of National University of Singapore (NUS) supervised by Prof. Mike Zheng Shou. I also work closely with Prof. Long Chen from HKUST and Prof. Na Zhao from SUTD. My research interest includes but not limited to 3D Generaton and Editing, Unbiased Learning and Vision-Language Understanding.
